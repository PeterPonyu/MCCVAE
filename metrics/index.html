Evaluation Metrics

Comprehensive metrics for assessing latent space quality and dimensionality reduction performance

LSE

Latent Space Evaluator

SingleCellLatentSpaceEvaluator

Evaluates latent space quality for single-cell data, designed for trajectory and steady-state data analysis.

Data Types:
trajectorysteady_state

DRE

Dimensionality Reduction Evaluator

DimensionalityReductionEvaluator

Evaluates dimensionality reduction quality by measuring structure preservation between the latent space and its 2D visualization projection (UMAP or t-SNE). This metric assesses how well the visualization preserves the original latent space structure.

Metrics:3

Clustering Metrics

Clustering Quality Evaluator

Clustering quality metrics computed on latent representations to assess how well the learned representations support cell type separation.

Metrics:4

Evaluator Comparison

LSE

  • Focus: Intrinsic latent space properties
  • Input: Latent space only
  • Use: Evaluating VAE/autoencoder latent representations

DRE

  • Focus: Structure preservation between latent space and visualization
  • Input: Latent space + UMAP/t-SNE projection
  • Use: Evaluating how well visualizations represent the latent space

Clustering

  • Focus: Cluster quality in latent space
  • Input: Latent representations + cluster labels
  • Use: Evaluating clustering performance
LSE

Latent Space Evaluation Metrics

manifold_dimensionalityCore Manifold

Manifold Dimensionality Score

Evaluates how efficiently the latent space captures the intrinsic dimensionality using variance thresholds, Kaiser criterion, elbow method, and spectral decay.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Higher indicates more efficient dimension usage
spectral_decay_rateSpectral Analysis

Spectral Decay Rate

Measures how quickly eigenvalues decay. Fast decay means information is concentrated in fewer dimensions.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Higher indicates information concentration in primary dimensions
participation_ratioSpectral Analysis

Participation Ratio Score

Quantifies how many dimensions effectively participate in the variance. Based on the ratio of squared sum to sum of squares of eigenvalues.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Trajectory: low (concentrated). Steady-state: high (distributed)
Data type dependent
anisotropy_scoreSpectral Analysis

Isotropy/Anisotropy Score

Measures directional uniformity using log-ellipticity, condition numbers, ratio variance, entropy, primary dominance, and effective dimension.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Trajectory: high anisotropy (strong direction). Steady-state: high isotropy (uniform)
Data type dependent
trajectory_directionalitySingle-Cell Specific

Trajectory Directionality Score

Evaluates the dominance of the primary developmental axis by comparing the first principal component's variance to others.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Higher indicates a clear developmental trajectory
noise_resilienceTechnical Quality

Noise Resilience Score

Evaluates how well the dimensionality reduction filters technical noise by computing signal-to-noise ratio from principal components.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Higher indicates good noise filtering
DRE

Dimensionality Reduction Metrics

Projection-based Evaluation: DRE metrics compare the latent space against UMAP or t-SNE projections to measure how well visualizations preserve the original structure.

Supported projections: UMAP, t-SNE

distance_correlationGlobal Structure

Distance Correlation

Spearman correlation between pairwise distances in the latent space and the 2D projection (UMAP/t-SNE). Measures how well global distance relationships are preserved in the visualization.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Higher (close to 1) indicates the projection faithfully preserves relative distances from the latent space
Q_localLocal Structure

Local Quality (Q_local)

Average quality score for neighborhood preservation in the projection, derived from the co-ranking matrix comparing latent space and projected neighborhoods.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Higher indicates local neighborhood structure is well preserved in the visualization
Q_globalGlobal Structure

Global Quality (Q_global)

Average quality score measuring large-scale structure fidelity between latent space and its projection.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Higher indicates global structure is well preserved in the visualization
CLU

Clustering Metrics

ASWCluster Separation

Average Silhouette Width

Measures how similar each sample is to its own cluster compared to other clusters.

Range:-1 to 1
Interpretation:Higher indicates well-separated clusters. Values near 0 indicate overlapping clusters.
CALCluster Validity

Calinski-Harabasz Index

Ratio of between-cluster dispersion to within-cluster dispersion. Also known as the Variance Ratio Criterion.

Range:0 to infinity
Interpretation:Higher indicates denser and well-separated clusters.
DAVCluster Compactness

Davies-Bouldin Index

Average similarity ratio of each cluster with its most similar cluster. Lower values indicate better clustering.

Range:0 to infinity
Interpretation:Lower indicates more compact and well-separated clusters.
CORDisentanglement

Correlation Measure

Average absolute off-diagonal correlation between latent dimensions. Measures independence of learned latent factors.

Range:0-1
Interpretation:Lower indicates better disentanglement (more independent latent dimensions).
